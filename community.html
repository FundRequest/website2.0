COMMUNITY, WHAT’S IN IT FOR YOU?

As a user and supporter of open source, you want to have an impact on the development of the tools you use. Whether you actively help solve bugs, program new features, or actively participate in discussions on online forums, your input matters.

WHAT’S IN IT FOR YOU?

FundRequest wants to give you even more power to participate in open source and the software development community. Our solution allows individual users to fund specific issues, or put a reward on features they want to see implemented.

  • Reward contributors
    Developers get paid for their contributions to open-source and other software projects.
  • Support the community
    By funding requests and rewarding individual developers, you also support the open-source and free software communities as a whole.
  • A secure and safe payment
    Since FundRequest is built on the Ethereum blockchain and uses open source Smart contracts, mutual trust and a secure transaction are guaranteed.
  • Build the software you want to see
    By proposing or funding new features, you have a hand in the development of the software.

WHY, WHAT, HOW

As a user of open source software, you want to see the community of developers thrive, and you want to help out any way you can.

FundRequest is a decentralised and open solution that makes it possible to fund only specific issues that are of value to you. You can issue a reward for any bug you want to see fixed or feature you want to see implemented. This reward goes straight to the developers resolving the issue – without any mediation of a middle man.

LEARN MORE

HOW TO FUND A REQUEST

  • Create a request based on a feature you want to see or a bug you’ve encountered, or find a request that you want to support.
  • Add your fund to the reward by clicking the “fund button”. The smart contract registers your account and adds your fund to the reward.
  • The most suitable solution is determined.
  • The developer gets paid: After the issue is solved, all funds are transferred to the contributor who offered the best solution.
