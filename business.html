WHAT’S IN IT FOR YOU?

FundRequest intends to create a market for projects with high business value but little or no professional customer support. Companies can use FundRequest to build a custom support package, tailored to the needs of their customers - a “Pay-As-You-Fix” model.

  • A secure and trustworthy payment system
    Thanks to the Ethereum blockchain and open source Smart contracts, trust and security are guaranteed.
  • A custom(er) support
    FundRequest bridges the gap between a total lack of support, and expensive service on an enterprise level. You only pay for problems that arise, or changes that are on your wishlist.
  • Pay for what you want
    By issueing or funding new features, you have an active say in the improvement of your preferred software tools.
  • Support the community
    By making sure individual developers get paid, you support the whole open-source and free software communities in one go – which further benefits the development of your favorite office tools.

WHY, WHAT, HOW

As a company that uses open source software, you are probably willing to pay for new features and bugfixes. However, not everyone can afford an expensive subscription for round-the-clock support.

FundRequest is a decentralised and open solution that makes it possible to fund only specific issues that are of value to you. You can issue a reward for any bug you want to see fixed or feature you want to see implemented.

Since we use the Ethereum network as our blockchain backend, FundRequest will be decentralized, secure and failproof.

HOW TO FUND A REQUEST

  • Create a request based on a feature you want to see or a bug you’ve encountered.
  • Add a reward to the request by clicking the “fund button”.
  • The smart contract is generated: The amount is sent to the Ethereum blockchain for validation, and a smart contract is generated for the specific issue.
  • The most suitable solution is determined.
  • The developer gets paid: After the issue is solved, all funds are transferred to the contributor who offered the best solution.
